About Us

Built for students who think in precedents.

The Legal Studies Club is a student-run organization dedicated to deepening our understanding of law — not just as a career path, but as a framework for thinking critically about society, justice, and governance.

We host speakers, organize moot court competitions, and create a space where intellectual debate is welcomed. Whether you're pre-law, curious about policy, or just love a good argument — you belong here.

No prerequisites. No prior experience required. Just a genuine interest in how the law shapes the world around us.

Debate & Moot Court
Practice oral arguments and written briefs in a supportive, structured environment.
Guest Speaker Series
Hear directly from attorneys, judges, and legal scholars across diverse fields.
Case Study Discussions
Unpack landmark rulings and current cases with guided discussion each month.
Career Guidance
Networking, internship tips, and mentorship for students considering law school.
